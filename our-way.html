Help and FAQ'S

Who is FOAN?

FOAN Realties Ltd is a Pan African real estate company with a knack to continuously innovate in the entire real estate value chain thereby making it easy and accessible to everyone craving to patronize it.

We make real estate ownership and investment accessible to everyone in a way that is simple, secure and profitable. We achieve this by practicing excellence, continually innovating, doing business with integrity and always putting our customers first.

What does FOAN mean?

FOAN means Fair, Outstanding, Authentic and Noble.

Are the properties you sale genuine?

The properties we sale are very genuine and free from government acquisition, adverse claim or any form of encumbrance. Our integrity matters to us.

What title does your properties have?

It all varies from property to property. The legal or equitable title of the properties ranges from deed of assignment to governor's consent.

Where are your properties is located?

Our properties are located in Abuja, Lagos, Delta, Anambra, Imo, Enugu and still counting.

How does your transaction process work?

The transaction process flow are as follows:

  • Inspection and verification of the property (optional)
  • Payment for property
  • Collect/Print or download Contract of Sale and Receipt online
  • Sign the Contract of Sale and return a copy back to the office (physically or scan)
  • Payment for Survey and Documentation (Deed)
  • Physical Allocation of plot(s) and issuance of allocation letter
  • Collect Deed and Survey
  • Sign the Deed and return two copies back to the office physically
  • Pay Development Levy
  • If a house purchase, you get your keys and ownership documents after full payment
What is the development timeline of your Estates?

The development timeline for the estates is between 12-60 months subject to prompt payment of all fees.

Where and how do I make payment?

All cash payments should be made to FOAN REALTIES LTD designated bank(s) details. Otherwise, cheque(s) should be issued in favour of FOAN REALTIES LTD. We shall not accept any responsibility for any liability that may arise, as result of deviation from the above condition.
