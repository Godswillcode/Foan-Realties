Who We Are

FOAN Realties Ltd is a Pan African real estate company with a knack to continuously innovate in the entire real estate value chain thereby making it easy and accessible to everyone craving to patronise it.

Vision

To make real estate ownership and investment a recipe for peace and prosperity in Africa.

Mission

1. To democratise real estate ownership and investment through innovation and technology with best employees welfare, customers satisfaction and investors reward.

2. To pioneer creation of new cities, develop prestigious homes and build modern infrastructure that will attract development, investment and businesses.

3. To develop mini cities in rural communities across Africa using our Contemporary Social Housing Community Model (COSOHCOM).

4. To revolutionalize the real estate value chain through innovation and technology.

5. To provide partnership and franchising opportunities for real estate business ownership.
